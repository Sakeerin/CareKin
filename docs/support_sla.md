# CareKin Support SLA

## Channels

- In-app support tickets: `/support`
- LINE OA: external operational channel
- Email: external operational channel

## Launch SLA

| Priority | Examples | Target first response |
|---|---|---|
| Urgent | Billing blocked, privacy/data deletion request, caregiver cannot access critical workflow | Same business day |
| High | Repeated errors, reminder/report workflow blocked | 1 business day |
| Medium | Product questions, single workflow issue, setup help | 2 business days |
| Low | Feature requests, copy issues, non-blocking UX feedback | 3 business days |

## Triage process

1. Confirm requester identity and workspace.
2. Set ticket type and priority.
3. Record first response time when moving to `in_progress` or `waiting_customer`.
4. Add resolution notes before `resolved` or `closed`.
5. Create follow-up product work for repeated issues.

## Privacy and safety

- Never ask users to paste unnecessary health information into support tickets.
- For deletion/export/consent requests, verify authority before action.
- Escalate medical-emergency language to a non-medical safety response: CareKin is not an emergency service; contact local emergency services or healthcare professionals.
