# CareKin Sales Assets

## Demo video script

1. Open with the caregiver problem: fragmented reminders, check-ins, vitals, and family communication.
2. Show onboarding: workspace, elder profile, consent, emergency contacts.
3. Show daily workflow: medication/routine reminder, task confirmation, missed escalation.
4. Show health workflow: daily check-in, vital entry, alert acknowledgement.
5. Show report workflow: generate weekly report, review AI draft, export/share with expiry.
6. Close with launch offer: family plans and provider plan.

Use `/demo` as the public storyboard and `/prototype/*` routes for safe recording without production data.

## B2B deck outline

1. Problem and market: family coordination around elder care.
2. Current pilot evidence: completion, report usage, feedback, willingness-to-pay.
3. Product walkthrough: reminders, check-ins, vitals, alerts, reports.
4. Safety and privacy: consent, audit log, expiring shares, non-clinical boundary.
5. Launch offer: provider onboarding, cohort metrics, SLA.
6. Pricing hypothesis: per family, per elder, or provider cohort package.
7. Next step: 2-week setup, 4-week paid launch pilot, decision review.

## One-pager claims to avoid

- Do not claim diagnosis, treatment recommendations, or emergency monitoring.
- Do not claim clinical outcomes without evidence.
- Do not imply AI summaries are medically verified unless reviewed by an authorized professional.
